Behind the Code

100% Self-Taught

My name is Thando Lameck Ncube — but on the internet, I go by Stellen. I'm a full stack developer based in South Africa who fell in love with building things on the web.

I didn't take the traditional route. No bootcamp, no university. Just curiosity, late nights, and an internet connection. I taught myself everything from HTML fundamentals to backend architecture and deployment pipelines.

I believe passion and perseverance matter more than credentials. Every project I build is a chance to level up and create something real that people can use.
